Streetbikes promotion video

The owner of the company Streetbikes BV had requested an exciting, yet informative video in order to promote their services. There was also the goal of making it look friendly and open towards perhaps new customers.

Concept

The owner shared the video on Facebook . It had been liked 62 times and 22 people commented. However the video had also been shared 24 times meaning people wanted to help share the message! The goal was to share the serviced and the crew from the company to the people and make it friendly and open towards the new customers. This goal had been reached.
